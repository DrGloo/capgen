About

CapGen is the perfect tool for you if you need a capton for your picture(s) Based upon your mood or just if you want a random quote from a random song, CapGen gives you a caption for your picture. Now you dont have to go looking for a quote.

There is a large library of songs and quotes. Once your caption is generated you can see the song in which the quote is from and the artist and you have an option to be sent to the artist's Spotify,SoundCloud or Youtube Page.
